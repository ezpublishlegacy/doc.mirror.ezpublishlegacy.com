Q: How can I contribute to eZ Publish's development?

A: All you need to do is go to the eZ Community Portal and get involved! Start on the "Download and Develop" pages where you can find all the information you need to get active in developing the eZ Publish Community Edition.

Before you can start your contribution be aware that you will need to sign an CLA agreement with eZ Systems. This agreement is described in detail in on the "Contributor Licensing Agreement-CLA" pages.

You can also contribute your ideas and feedback on the Community Portal forums.
