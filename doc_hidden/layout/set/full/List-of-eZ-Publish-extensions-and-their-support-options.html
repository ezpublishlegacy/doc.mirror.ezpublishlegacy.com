List of eZ Publish extensions and their support options

Professionally supported extensions

The table below lists extensions and their support options. "Included in a standard agreement" is either "eZ Publish Network", "eZ Publish Premium", and "eZ Publish Enterprise", or "Dedicated".

"Included in partner agreement" is either "SMB", "Partner Shared Subscription", or "Shared". "Requires extra agreement" means that no matter where it comes from (distro, setup, manual download), it requires an extra support subscription in order to receive support. "Dependencies" lists other extensions that are kind of libraries to the marketed extensions.
