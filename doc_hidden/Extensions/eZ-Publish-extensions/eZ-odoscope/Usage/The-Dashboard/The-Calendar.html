The Calendar

The Calendar

The Calendar is very important when it comes to creating reports, as you select the time period you want to analyze on it.

If you click on the arrow at the left side of the word "Date", you will be able to use the odoscope Calendar.

The Calendar allows you to set the time period that you want to use to generate your report(s) from months, weeks and days.

eZ odoscope Calendar

You can broaden or narrow the desired time period by clicking on the little blue rectangles available on the right and left sides of the rectangular figure shown in the picture above, and moving them from right to left and from left to right. If you double click inside the rectangle, it will expand and add more days/weeks/months to the time period. The new time period selected will be shown in the blue area at the bottom of the figure, and your report(s)/chart(s) will change, according to the data available for the time period chosen.

Important: The "Time Period" is  the first filtering level on eZ odoscope. The second filtering level is the Filtergroup, and the third filtering level is the  Usergroup. When you change any of this filtering levels, your reports will automatically adapt to the new filtering selected.

The Events Manager

The odoscope Calendar helps you set up your agenda with the Events Manager.

If you double click on the little squares shown under the calendar (which represent a day of the week), you will be able to schedule an event for a specific day/time:

eZ odoscope Calendar Appointments

Write a Subject, a Description, and select the Start and End Time of the event. If it is an "All day event", click on the corresponding check box.

Other actions you can perform on this screen:

  • Edit the Recurrence of the event:
    • Recurrence Pattern: Daily, Weekly, Monthly, Days of the Week
    • Recur Every (n) Weeks
    • Range of Recurrence: Start, No End Date, End After (n) Recurrence(s), End By (specific date)
  • Choose how you want to show the appointment (Time makers):
    • Free
    • Tentative
    • Busy
    • Out of Office
  • Choose the Category of  the appointment: by clicking on the corresponding drop-down box, which will show several colours for you to choose the one that fits best for your event;
  • Prioritize the appointment: by choosing between the red circle with the exclamation point, or the black down arrow.

The event will be added to your time line.

eZ odoscope Calendar Events

Note: To delete an event, click on it and confirm the action.
