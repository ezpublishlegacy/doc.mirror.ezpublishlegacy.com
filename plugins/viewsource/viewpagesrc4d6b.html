September 12th 2016

eZ announces the availability of 5.3.10, a maintenance release available for all users of eZ Publish Platform 5.3.

Follow the 5.3.x Update Instructions to apply this update on your platforms. If you're migrating from 5.3.10, a simple composer update ezsystems/ezpublish-kernel should be sufficient to update.
