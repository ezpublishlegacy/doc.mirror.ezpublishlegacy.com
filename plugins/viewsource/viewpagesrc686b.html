eZ Enterprise is a commercial addition and extension to eZ Platform. It makes use of everything that the platform has to offer, and expands it capabilities.

eZ Enterprise contains a new intuitive, easy-to-learn editing interface called Studio UI, but also adds new functionalities to the existing interfaces. The main features available in eZ Enterprise are:

  • Inline editing of content
  • Creating Landing Pages with customizable blocks
  • Scheduling automatic publication of content on Landing Pages
  • Content review workflow
