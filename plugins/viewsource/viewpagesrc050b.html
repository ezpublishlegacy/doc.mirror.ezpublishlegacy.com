Welcome to the Technical Documentation of the eZ Publish Platform 5.x

This documentation covers functionality in eZ Publish 5.x, a hybrid version of eZ Publish CMS containing eZ Publish 4.x "Legacy", and "Platform" a new generation of eZ Publish on top of Symfony 2 Full stack framework. For further reading on the difference between Platform and legacy, and how they are both bundled in eZ Publish 5.x versions, see eZ Publish 5 Architecture - Introduction & Overview.
