A FieldType is the smallest possible entity of storage. It determines how a specific type of information should be validated, stored, retrieved and formatted.

eZ Publish comes with a collection of fundamental datatypes that can be used to build powerful and complex content structures. In addition, it is possible to extend the system by creating custom FieldTypes for special needs. Custom FieldTypesÂ have to be programmed in PHP.
